Join the ACED Cohort

Thank you for considering joining the ACED Cohort. If you are between 40 and 80, have never had cancer or completed any treatment for cancer over a year ago, and are willing to consider participating in early cancer detection research, please take a look at this website and fill in the form below to register your interest. Someone from our team will contact you soon.

On joining the ACED Cohort, you will be asked to complete a questionnaire and provide a blood and urine sample. You will then be assessed and contacted if you are eligible to participate in any further research studies on early cancer detection.
